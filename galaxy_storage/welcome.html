SYNBIOCHEM PIPELINE

  1. DeS2DoE Generate JMP DoE instructions from a Design sheet.
  2. DoE2LiB Generate constructs list from DoE library.
  3. PlasmidGenie Generate order list for parts and oligos and plasmids list from constructs.
  4. PlasmidWriter Write plasmids in ICE.
  5. StrainWriter Write strains in ICE.
  6. PrimerTool Generate primer order list from plasmids.
  7. PrimerPlate Convert primer list into plate list.
  8. EnzymeScreening Generate worklist for enzyme screening from assays.
  9. LCR2Assembly Generate worklists for LCR2 assembly from plasmids and plates.
